MY ROLE

User interview, storyboard drawing, prototyping, user interface design.

Our Topic

Help students maintain focus

Many students rely on electric devices during the pandemic to have online classes.

However, these devices also distract students from online classes. Students might browse social media, video platforms, or other things that do not relate to study.

Our main goal is to find a method to help students stay focused during the online class.
