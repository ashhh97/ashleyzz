Project 1: ScriptManager (On-going project)

ScriptManager is an opensource software written by JAVA for for analyzing sequencing data and generating figures. It contains 40 tools for genomics analysis. It includes graphical interface for non-programmer users and the command line interface. The product will lanuch in July. The feedback will be given by then.

My role

3. Epigenomics Facility workflow/website design and development.

The EGC provides the service that maps protein-DNA interactions genome-wide, tracks experimental metadata, and implements quality-controlled data processing and research-based analysis pipelines. (from EGC website)

The original text workflow

The professor received a growing complaint from her customers that the service workflow is discontinuous. Customers always ask her what is the next step. Professor provides me with the text version of the workflow and ask me to simplify and visualize the workflow, so that the customers could read through the workflow easily without asking her.

The refined workflow

We worked together and revised the workflow several times, and mockup the workflow in Figma first. I broke the steps into 6 large steps, and each step contains several small steps. So that the users will not be tired of reading so many words.

The workflow in figma

After the team approved, I start to develop WordPress. But the Cornell WordPress doesn’t support tab function, and the format needs to be uniform to another lab’s page. So, I changed the design. The finalized design can be viewed at EGC website.
