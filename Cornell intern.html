    EGC Website design/development

    Background

    The EGC provides the service that maps protein-DNA interactions genome-wide, tracks experimental metadata, and implements quality-controlled data processing and research-based analysis pipelines. (Reference: EGC Website)

    Problem

    The professor received a growing complaint from her customers that the service workflow is discontinuous. And the text workflow is too long. Customers always ask her what is the next step. Professor provides me with the text version of the workflow and ask me to simplify and visualize the workflow, so that the customers could read through the workflow easily without asking her.

    My design:

    We worked together and revised the workflow several times, and mockup the workflow in Figma first. I broke the steps into 6 large steps, and each step contains several small steps. So that the users will not be tired of reading so many words.
