Online Pet

06/01/2019-07/01/2019

This app aims at helping stray animals by sharing stray animal videos, attracting donations. By making a profit from putting advertisements, this app will reward high clicking videos. Shelters' accounts could also help sell a product to make a profit to continue running the shelters.

Background

  • There are 50 million stray cats and dogs in China every year.

  • The number is increasing by 1.5 times every year.

  • Currently, Chinaâ€™s stray animal shelters are organized by individuals.

  • Most of them are lack funds.

  • They need more financial support.

  • There is a phenomenon of "raising pets" online in China.

  • People who cannot have a pet tend to watch pets' short videos online.

  • We can help stray animals by using this phenomenon.

01

Survey

conclusion: Most people in the survey love pets, but they cannot have one for some reasons. They often watch online pets reels to fulfill themselves. People who love pets tend to sympathize with stray animals, and some are willing to donate money to stray animals.

User Interface

Pet call

On "My Page," users can see the pets they adopt online and make a video call to see their situation.

Donate
In the online shopping section, people who have pets can buy pets commodities here. People who do not have a pet can buy pets, food, or other good pets and donate to the stray animals shelter directly.
Home Page

Scrolling the screen to see animals' short videos, double click the "Home" to back to the top.

Click the cover of the video; users can play the video. Users can like, share, comment, donate the video on the video interface, and add it to their favorite to watch it repeatedly.

The higher the clicking rate that the video gets, the more money the video launcher can get from the "Help stray animals" platform so that video launchers can use the funds to save stray animals.
